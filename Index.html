About Me

Hi, I’m Felix! I’m currently studying in the Art&Tech department. I have a keen interest in interaction design and hope to work in UI/UX design or HMI design in the future. I play the harmonica, and I enjoy playing badminton and archery. I also follow F1 racing. Let’s grab a coffee sometime!☕

Questions I Explore

  1. As an Art&Tech artist, should I focus on accumulating technical skills or artistic skills first?
  2. Will more advanced technology necessarily help with my artistic expression?
  3. Will technological advancements affect the value of art creation?
  4. Should I follow trendy technologies and develop multiple skills, or focus on one or a few technologies and continue to develop them?
  5. Should I promote my work on social media?
  6. How can I help more people understand my work?
  7. Is widespread recognition necessary to be considered successful?
  8. How can I maintain my personal style and originality when using technology in art creation?
  9. How can I evaluate the impact of technology on the quality of art creation?
  10. Will the proliferation of technology lead to homogenization in art creation? How can this be avoided?
  11. When choosing technology tools, how do I weigh their practical value in art creation against their learning costs?
  12. How can I assess the audience’s acceptance of the combination of technology and art? Will this affect my creation?
  13. Is it necessary to understand the scientific principles behind technology to enhance the depth of art creation?
  14. When using new technology in art creation, how can I ensure the long-term value and durability of the work?
  15. If technology issues affect my creation, should I spend more time learning new technology or compromise by using older technology with less effective results?
  16. In technology-driven art creation, how can I ensure that the emotional and humanistic aspects of the work are not overlooked?
  17. Can my work be created solely to please myself?
  18. How significant are the differences in results when using different expressive forms for the same idea?
  19. Will VR and AR technologies become mainstream?
  20. When using the same technology, how can I ensure that my work does not become too formulaic?
  21. How can I maintain creativity and inspiration when encountering technical problems in the creative process?
  22. Will the combination of technology and art give rise to entirely new forms of art?
  23. If the introduction of technology results in unexpected effects in my creation, should I stick to my original idea?
  24. How can I ensure that the aesthetic value of my work is not overshadowed by technical details?
  25. How can I address the issue of over-reliance on technology in art creation?
